Our Work & Projects

Discover the initiatives we've undertaken to change lives.

Food Items

To combat hunger and ensure food security, we distribute packages of essential food items to families in need. Each package contains staples like flour, rice, lentils, and cooking oil, providing crucial sustenance. This initiative is vital for supporting families facing economic hardship, allowing them to have nutritious meals on their tables.

  • Distribution of monthly food packages to over 300 families.
  • Special food drives during Ramadan and other holidays.
  • Targeted support for widows, orphans, and daily wage earners.

Qurbaani (Sacrifice)

During the festival of Eid-ul-Adha, our Qurbaani program ensures that the meat from sacrificed animals reaches the most impoverished families. This act of faith and charity allows many who cannot afford meat throughout the year to partake in the blessings of Eid. We manage the entire process, from sourcing healthy animals to distributing the meat hygienically.

  • Distributed meat to over 1,000 families last Eid.
  • Ensured proper and humane handling of all animals.
  • Reached remote communities to share the joy of Eid.

Blanket Distribution

Warmth is a basic necessity, especially during harsh winters. Our blanket distribution program ensures that poor and vulnerable families are protected from the cold by providing them with quality blankets. This small act of kindness brings comfort, safety, and relief to those who struggle the most during the winter season.

  • Distributed over 500 blankets last winter.
  • Focus on widows, orphans, and low-income families.
  • Campaigns launched every winter to reach remote and underserved areas.

Business Scheme

We believe in empowerment through self-sufficiency. Our Business Scheme provides individuals with the assets and initial capital to start their own small businesses. This allows them to earn a sustainable livelihood with dignity. We assist with setting up Karyana (grocery) stores, tuc shops, and provide assets like rickshaws.

  • Helped launch over 40 small businesses.
  • Provided 15 rickshaws for transport businesses.
  • Ongoing support and training for new entrepreneurs.
